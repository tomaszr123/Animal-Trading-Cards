Black Cockatoo

In captivity, the black cockatoo has a lifespan similar to humans.

  • Scientific Name: Calyptorhynchus banksii
  • Species: There are six different types of Black Cockatoos.
  • Average Lifespan: 45 to 50 years.
  • Habitat: Eucalyptus forests or Woodlands.

The Red-Tailed Black-Cockatoo is mostly glossy black and has an erectile crest which forms an obvious helmet when raised and pushed forward.
